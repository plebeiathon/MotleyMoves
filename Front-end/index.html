Join our Biweekly Training Events

Modesto Moves Training Team is a non-profit organization, providing formal training for 5K, 10K, half and full marathons. Our 250 particpants train at all levels of walking and running along with RRCA certified coaches and mentors, volunteering to help the community to stay moving and get healthy.

Connect with Coaches and Mentors

Our coaches are Road Runners Club of America certified to coach you from where ever you are to where ever you want to be. Our coaches have years of experience in all levels from 5Ks to ultra-marathons. We have six levels of experienced coaches, from rolling to walking and running.
